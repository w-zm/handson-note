## 数学公式

$$ RMSE(X, h)=\sqrt{\frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^m(h(x^{(i)}-y^{(i)})^2} $$          //Root Mean Saure Error

$$MAE(X, h) = \frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^m|h(x^{(i)}-y^{(i)})|$$                  //Mean Absolute Error
